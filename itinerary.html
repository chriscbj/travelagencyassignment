Day 1 SINGAPORE - INCHEON - SEOUL

Assemble at Singapore Changi Airport for your flight to Incheon. Upon arrival, proceed for PCR test and transfer to hotel for check-in.

Day 2 SEOUL

Breakfast/ Korean Set Lunch/ Hot Stone Pot Bibimbap Dinner

Accommodation: Novotel Ambassador Seoul Yongsan or Best Western Premier Seoul Garden Hotel

Day 3 SEOUL - INCHEON - JEJU

This morning, visit the majestic Gyeongbok Palace, the palace during the Joseon dynasty. Here you will also get to don on traditional Korean dress and stroll through the palace ground in a Hanbok dressup. Thereafter, transfer to the airport for your domestic flight to Jeju.

Accommodation: Ocean Suites Jeju Hotel or similar

Day 4 JEJU

This morning, enjoy a scenic ride to the beautiful Woljeongri Beach windmill coastal road. Next gear up for a thrilling all-terrain vehicle ride. Next, stop at a local fruit farm and enjoy picking and tasting tangerines. Later, visit the magnificent Jusangjeolli Cliff, which was formed when the lava from Hallasan erupted into the sea of Jungmun. After which, proceed to Seonimgyo Bridge, also known as the Seven Nymphs Bridge. Take a relaxing stroll across the bridge and be mesmerised by its beautiful scenery. Thereafter, head to Camellia Hill where your heart melts and your dormant passion comes back to life as you gaze upon the red camellia flowers blooming in the cold winter.

Accommodation: Ocean Suites Jeju Hotel or similar

Day 5 JEJU - BUSAN - DAEGU

Transfer to the airport for your domestic flight to Busan. Upon arrival, visit Busan Gamcheon Culture Village. Wander along colourful streets covered with wall murals. Next, head for some heart-stopping action at Oryukdo Skywalk, floating 40 metres above the surface of the water. End your day at Gukje Market, one of Korea’s largest traditional markets. Stay overnight in Daegu.

Accommodation: Hotel Inter-Burgo Daegu

Day 6 DAEGU - PYEONGCHANG

Breakfast/ Dried Pollack Set Lunch

Begin your day with a relaxing coach ride to Pyeongchang. Upon arrival, we will check-in to Yongpyong resort. Afterwhich, enjoy a scenic cable car ride to Mount Balwang where you can experience the breath-taking Skywalk. At Mount Balwang, you will also find white powder snow with breath-taking view of winter. Later in the afternoon, indulge in a host of fun snow activities. You will also receive a complimentary pair Ski gloves.

Day 7 PYEONGCHANG - SINGAPORE

(Light Breakfast at Airport/ Meals On Board)
This morning, transfer to the airport for your flight home.
